Infosys


Infosys Limited is an Indian multinational information technology company that provides business consulting, informationtechnology and outsourcing services. The company was founded in Pune and is headquartered inBangalore. Infosys is the second-largest Indian IT company, after Tata Consultancy Services, by 2020 revenue figures, and the 602nd largest public company in the world, according to the Forbes Global 2000 ranking On 24 August 2021, Infosys became the fourth Indian company to reach $100 billion in market capitalization.
